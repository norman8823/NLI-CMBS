## Executive Summary

BANK5 2024-5YR11 is a $949.1M conduit transaction secured by 94 first-lien commercial mortgage loans with a single-vintage maturity in 2030. The pool carries a WA coupon of 6.3% and WA DSCR of 1.38x with zero delinquencies as of the March 2026 reporting period. The concentrated maturity wall — 100.0% of UPB maturing in 2030 — represents the dominant risk factor for this transaction.

## Deal Performance Overview

Current UPB totals $949.1M against an original UPB of $949.1M, consistent with a newly issued pool. The WA coupon is 6.3% and WA DSCR is 1.38x. WA occupancy is 92.8%.

IO loans represent 36.2% of the pool (34 loans). Balloon loans comprise 37.2% of the pool. Property type composition is diversified: Multifamily at 27.8% of UPB ($263.8M, 13 loans), Retail at 26.6% ($252.2M, 13 loans), Lodging at 17.1% ($162.6M, 9 loans), Manufactured Housing at 11.0% ($104.4M, 26 loans), Self Storage at 10.6% ($100.8M, 31 loans), and Office at 6.9% ($65.3M, 2 loans).

The 26 Manufactured Housing loans, while numerous, average only $4.0M per loan. The two Office loans average $32.7M and represent concentrated single-asset exposure.

## Delinquency & Special Servicing

The pool reports zero delinquencies across all buckets (30-day, 60-day, 90+ day) as of March 2026. No loans are specially serviced. No loans are in foreclosure or REO.

Seasoning remains minimal. Credit deterioration, if it materializes, is most likely to emerge from the Lodging segment ($162.6M, 9 loans), which is sensitive to macroeconomic cycles and travel demand.

## Maturity & Refinancing Risk

All 94 loans in the pool mature in calendar year 2030, creating a single-year maturity wall of $949.1M (100.0% of UPB):

- **2030**: 94 loans totaling $949.1M

This concentrated maturity profile means the entire pool faces refinancing risk simultaneously. The November-December 2030 window alone accounts for the majority of scheduled maturities. Interest rate conditions and CRE capital availability in 2030 will determine the refinancing outcome for every loan in the pool.

No loans mature within the next 24 months.

## Outlook

BANK5 2024-5YR11 is performing with zero delinquencies and adequate WA DSCR at 1.38x. The single-year 2030 maturity wall ($949.1M, 100.0% of UPB) is the defining risk for this transaction — refinancing conditions in 2030 will be the sole determinant of pool credit outcomes. Key items to monitor include the Lodging segment's cyclical sensitivity, below-WA DSCR on the two largest Retail exposures, and emerging seasoning patterns over the next four quarters.
